Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Is Pelican compatible with my existing configurations?

Yes, Pelican is designed to ensure optimal compatibility with existing Pterodactyl configurations. Migration tools are provided to facilitate the transition.

What are the main differences with Pterodactyl?

Pelican offers a completely redesigned architecture, delivering increased performance, simplified maintenance, and exclusive features. Updates are also more frequent.

Is the solution truly faster?

Yes, benchmarks show response times up to 10 times faster for common operations, thanks to advanced code and resource optimization.

Are there any additional fees?

No, Pelican is a free open-source solution. Premium services such as personalized support may be offered as options, but the core project remains accessible at no cost.

How can I get support?

An active community is available on Discord, complemented by comprehensive documentation. For specific needs, professional support is also available.
